About Eclipse

Our Story

Welcome to Eclipse, a cutting-edge clothing brand that blends style and comfort to bring you the latest fashion trends. Founded by Joey Peckumn, Eclipse is more than just a brand; it's a statement of individuality and confidence.

Founder - Joey Peckumn

Joey Peckumn, the creative force behind Eclipse, has always been passionate about fashion and self-expression. With a vision to redefine the fashion industry, Joey embarked on a journey to create a brand that resonates with those who seek unique and stylish apparel.

Our Mission

At Eclipse, our mission is to empower individuals to express their identity through fashion. We believe that clothing is a powerful form of self-expression, and we strive to provide you with high-quality, trendsetting pieces that reflect your style and personality.
